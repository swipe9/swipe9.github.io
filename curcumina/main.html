    ABOUT CURCUMINA

    Curcumin is the key ingredient which gives turmeric its magical properties. While most of the turmeric available in the market has 1.5 to 2 percent Curcumin, the CURCUMINA organic turmeric from village Waigaon has about 6 percent, four times that of others. The unique soil and weather conditions of Waigaon give their turmeric this property. Further, to ensure high Curcumin content, it is hand pounded and dried in shade. Waigaon Turmeric has been awarded the GI (Geographical Index) registration #471 just like Deogad Alphonso and Nagpur Oranges.

    How we do it?

    Curcumina India has a direct association with members of Waigaon Halad Producer Company, a corporation of farmers founded by our director. Further, these farmers are closed guided and supervised to grow their turmeric as per strict Organic Guidelines.

    After the turmeric powder is produced, every batch is tested in a government approved laboratory to ensure high Curcumin content. It is also tested to make sure about absence of harmful ingredients like EColi and Mercury. The powder is then hygienically packed into sealed bottles or further processed to make tablets/capsules and then packed.
